Design Guidelines

A premium, liquid-glass playbook for agentic products. Build calm interfaces with richer color composition, sharper hierarchy, and production-grade detail standards across desktop and mobile.

Calm Futurism Advanced Glass Operational Density Accessibility First

01 · Core Principles

Use color as hierarchy, glass as depth, spacing as rhythm, and motion as context. Beauty must improve decision speed.

P1

Color With Intention

Main content stays neutral. Accent colors only mark actions, status, and attention priority.

P2

Depth Without Noise

Layer by blur, translucency, and edge highlights before adding stronger contrast or hue shifts.

P3

Compact Clarity

Dashboards should be dense but readable: short titles, tight rows, and stable scan paths.

P4

Predictable Interaction

Hover lifts are subtle, focus is explicit, and transitions stay fast and fluid.
